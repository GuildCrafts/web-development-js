### 11: Lairotcaf

The factorial operator (!) describes the product of an integer and all the integers below it.

```4! -> 4 * 3 * 2 * 1 -> 24```

Write a function that does the reverse of this for any positive integer.

**Example Inputs:**

`720`

`37`

**Example Output:**

`720 = 6!`

`37 NONE`
